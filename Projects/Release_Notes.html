Purpose

The FP-ATR-BLE1 The FP-ATR-BLE1 is STM32Cube Function Pack for the Pro Mode of the:
  • SensorTile.box PRO discovery box with multi-sensors and wireless connectivity for any intelligent IoT node(STEVAL-MKBOXPRO)

The purpose of this functional pack is to provide simple asset tracking application for STEVAL-MKBOXPRO Pro that could be controlled by Bluetooth and NFC connectivity.
